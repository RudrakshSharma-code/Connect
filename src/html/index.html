About Us
We are four students from BCIT working together to help reduce the spread of Covid-19. We want to do our part in the fight against Covid-19 by helping those who need it most.
Our Goal
Our goal is to give a platform for people that are unable to leave thier house to get essential supplies due to the recent Covid-19 Pandemic can reach out for help. Through this app we hope that we help reduce the spread of the Covid-19, as well as help those that require help in this time of need.
